AP Forex DayTrader (MT5)

Intraday MT5 EA for major FX pairs. Combines a Momentum Pullback engine on M15 with a London session breakout box on M5. Validator-safe with clear risk controls.

EA MT5 Forex Intraday

Buy Now (MT5) Presets (.set)

Overview

  • Momentum Pullback: fast/slow MAs + RSI on M15 to find pullback entries with trend.
  • London Breakout Box: session box on M5 with buffered breakout entries.
  • Risk controls: % risk or fixed lots, ATR or fixed SL/TP, daily loss guard, max trades per day.
  • Execution: margin checks, fill-mode fallback, strict magic/comment filtering.
  • No martingale. No grid. No DLL/WebRequest.

Quick start

  1. Place the EA in MQL5\Experts\ and restart MT5.
  2. Attach AP Forex DayTrader (MT5) to a major FX pair (e.g. EURUSD) on M15.
  3. Click Inputs → Load and load one of the presets below.
  4. Enable Algo Trading and watch the Experts tab for any warnings.

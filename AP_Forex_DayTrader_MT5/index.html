Overview

Designed for liquid FX pairs (EURUSD, GBPUSD, USDJPY, etc.), AP Forex DayTrader focuses on structured intraday entries with strict risk controls and validator-clean order handling.

Honest expectations

  • No promises, no martingale/grid.
  • Meant for thoughtful testing and sensible risk.
  • Presets are starting points — always forward-test first.

Highlights

  • Session filters (London/NY) and daily trade guard.
  • Clean pending/market order logic with spread/stop checks.
  • Fixed-lots or money-risk sizing.
  • Readable on-chart labels and logs.

Compatibility

  • Platform: MetaTrader 5
  • Symbols: Major FX pairs
  • Timeframes: M5–H1 (presets provided)

Quick start

  1. Download a Preset (see button above) and load it via Inputs → Load.
  2. Attach to your chosen FX chart and timeframe.
  3. Check Experts tab for any broker-specific warnings (stops/spread).
  4. Forward-test on demo before considering live use.

Note: The Presets and Docs links will show 404 until you add those folders/files.

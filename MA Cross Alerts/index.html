MA Cross Alerts

Simple moving-average cross alerts with on-chart labels and optional notifications. Neutral styling and lightweight for everyday charts.

Get MA Cross Alerts (free)

Overview

  • Plots fast/slow MA and marks cross events (golden/death cross style).
  • Optional push/email/terminal alerts on cross; on-chart labels are minimal.
  • Works on any symbol/timeframe; designed to stay unobtrusive.

Quick Start

  1. Attach to your chart and set FastMAPeriod, SlowMAPeriod, and Method.
  2. Choose Price (close by default) and whether to show labels.
  3. Enable Alerts if you want notifications on cross.

Main Inputs (summary)

  • FastMAPeriod / SlowMAPeriod — lengths for the two MAs.
  • Method — SMA/EMA/SMMA/LWMA.
  • Price — applied price (close, open, median, etc.).
  • ShowLabels — on-chart cross markers.
  • EnableAlerts — push/email/terminal alerts on cross.
  • Colors/Widths — MA and label styling (kept neutral by default).

Notes

  • No trading signals/claims; it’s a visual alert tool only.
  • Support via MQL5 messages/comments only.

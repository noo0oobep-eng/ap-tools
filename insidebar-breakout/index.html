What it does

Detects inside bars and places pending orders with your buffer. SL can be on the opposite side; TP can be a fixed R multiple. Unfilled pendings auto-cancel the same day to keep things clean.

Open Strategy Tester Report

Key inputs

  • SignalTF — timeframe used for inside-bar detection
  • BufferPts — extra points above/below the trigger
  • SL_Mode — “OppositeSide” or fixed points
  • TP_R_Multiple — clean target in R
  • ExpireSameDay — cancel unfilled pendings before EOD
  • Magic — unique ID to avoid clashes

AP Round Numbers Grid (MT5)

Draws a round-number grid by step (points), with optional half/quarter levels. Non-trading and validator-safe.

Get the download

We’ll log your email via Formspree and then start a direct download of AP_Round_Numbers_Grid_MT5.zip. No extra pages.

What it does

  • Plots horizontal grid lines every step (points) across the visible chart.
  • Optional half (step/2) and quarter (step/4 and 3×step/4) levels.
  • Style controls: colour, width, line style (dot/dash), draw behind candles, hide/unselectable.
  • Non-trading: purely visual; no orders, no alerts. Very light on resources.

How to use

  1. Attach to any chart and set Step (points). On 5-digit FX, 500 ≈ 50 pips.
  2. Toggle Half and Quarter levels as needed.
  3. Adjust colours/width/style and housekeeping (back/hidden/unselectable).
  4. Use Max lines to keep it safe when zooming far out.

Tip: Find your symbol’s point size in “Symbol specification” to pick a sensible step.

Inputs

  • Step (points) — distance between main grid lines.
  • Show half levels — adds middle line between steps.
  • Show quarter levels — adds step/4 and 3×step/4 lines.
  • Main/Half/Quarter colors, Main/Minor widths, Style.
  • Back, Hidden, Unselectable — tidy UI options.
  • Max lines — hard cap for performance safety.
